Automatic Trash Can



Me and Jayden made a trash can that senses our movements and opnes by itself.
We used ultrasonic sensor for sensing, and straws for opening the lid.
We used cardboard for the main shape, and making it stable was hard.
It took a few trials to make it stable, but we made it at last.

Maqueen soccer science fair project

For my science fair project, I am working with Adhish and Noel to make a maqueen soccer.
We printed the goal and soccer ball with the 3d printer.
For the other parts, we mostly used cardboard and box.
In the microbit maqueen, we added a push module so that it can push the ball.
We added a string to the soccer goal so that the ball doesn't go out of the field.
